NEXT STEPS:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Create a Telegram bot with @BotFather
   - Open Telegram, search for @BotFather
   - Send /newbot
   - Follow prompts and copy the bot token

2. Start the setup process:
   $ ./telegram-setup.sh
   or
   $ pi

3. In Pi, configure the bot:
   /telegram-setup
   (paste bot token)

4. Connect the bridge:
   /telegram-connect

5. Pair your Telegram account:
   - Find your bot in Telegram
   - Send /start

6. Start chatting!
